SERVICES

VISA SUPPORT

Updated with the latest immigration laws and policies, our team will be standing with you throughout visa process. Right from preparing and filing documents till your final visa interview, we shall support you.

IELTS COACHING

IELTS (International English Language Testing System) measures the language ability of people who wish to study or work in an environment where English is the language of conversation. Those who desire university entrance, take the Academic Version and those who are looking to immigrate to an English-speaking country, typically sit the General Training module or SELT (Secure English Language Test) for the UK.

STUDENT COUNSELING

Our knowledgeable experts guide both adolescents and adults to explore various career options and assist in pursuing their goals to study abroad.

ADMISSION PROCESS

We assist and guide the students in selecting right course and university with worldwide recognition. Our experts help the students in filing error-free and well-presented application for admission in desired institutions.

PRE-DEPARTURE

Travelling abroad and getting settled in a foreign land is a bit difficult task for most of the youngsters. In our pre-departure services, we inform our candidates about critical information and familiarize them with practices that help them adopt the lifestyle of their new destination.

POST ARRIVAL

Our service does not end once you reach to your desired destination. We are still there to help you and assist to get you comfortable in settling to the new land. Right from pick-up from airport, temporary accommodation and assistance to find a part time job, we are around.
